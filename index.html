Christmas S.E.L. Missions

Santa's Jingle Cues

Body Check: Santa feels his tummy wiggling like jelly when he's nervous.

Mission: Where do you feel your feelings? Can you wiggle like jelly too?

The Labeling Reindeer

Feeling Names: Rudolph feels "Excited" to fly!

Mission: What is a big feeling you had today? Was it "Happy" or "Frustrated"?

Elf Workshop Breathing

Belly Breath: Elves stay calm by breathing deep while making toys.

Mission: Let's "Smell the Christmas Tree" (breathe in) and "Blow out the Candle" (breathe out).

Polar Bear's Toolbox

Self-Control: When Polar Bear gets mad, he goes to his snowy cave to count to 10.

Mission: What is your favorite way to stay calm? A hug or a deep breath?
